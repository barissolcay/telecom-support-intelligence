# DSL Troubleshooting Guide

Version 2.1 · Effective 2026-01-01 · Synthetic training material

## Periodic speed degradation

Compare results over Wi-Fi and Ethernet on the same device. Review SNR, synchronization rate, recent disconnections, and regional capacity alarms. If wired speed is normal, continue with Wi-Fi interference checks. If both wired and wireless speeds degrade during the same peak-hour window, investigate line quality and regional congestion.

## Safety

Do not promise field repair, capacity expansion, price adjustment, or SLA credit before the responsible team confirms eligibility.
